## Decision rules

Retain means the source recommendation survives materially intact; Merge combines overlapping sources; Rewrite changes scope/ownership/evidence/priority; Split creates independently deliverable outcomes; Convert to verification prevents implementation before evidence; Convert to ADR makes a governing decision precede code; Defer preserves a trigger; Reject records why the action is unsafe or unjustified; Replace uses a stronger alternative. No recommendation is silently omitted.


## Public evidence boundary

Private audit reconciliation decisions and raw evidence references are retained by maintainers and are not published here.
